What's it all about?

Bringing freecodecamp.com to Kids


Anyone can learn to code, sometimes it just takes a little help to get started. Freecodecamp.com has done an incredible job making the content available, now we're partnering with the Georgian government to bring it the last mile.

Join us as we help a group of secondary school students (aged 13-17) learn to creatively problem solve on a team while learning to build simple web applications using Javascript, HTML, and CSS.

What's the deal?

4 Weeks

Sept. 16 - Oct. 15, 2017. All expenses paid to come hang out at harvest time in beautiful Tbilisi, Georgia with an 8,000 year old wine culture, unique history, and a peaceful, rapidly modernizing society.

30 Kids

30 kids aged 13-17, all eager to explore and learn. They'll have varying and limited exposure to desktop computers, but plenty of curiosity and experience playing with smartphones.

Freecodecamp.com

No need to reinvent the wheel, we'll use freecodecamp.com to help kids work through the the learning process. They've compiled an amazing resource.
