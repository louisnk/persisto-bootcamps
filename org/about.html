A little about us

Persisto Learn is born out of gratitude for those who came before us, who gave freely of their time and knowledge to empower and enrich the lives of young people. It is with this gratitude we work to provide opportunities for youth in places less visited, where passion and creativity shine bright regardless of resources.

Today's youth were born into a world where technology is all consuming and its impact is everywhere. From a very young age they are competent users of technology, but rarely aware of the ease with which they can create it. By building on the great work of countless people and organizations before us, we can help young people cultivate their creativity and confidence to take on the challenges they will face.

The technology pioneers who envisioned and created the devices and networks we use today foresaw them being used for education and enrichment of communities. They dreamt that one of a user's first desires would be to learn and create, to explore the vast web of knowledge we call the internet. Although reality may differ from that dream, we've seen time and again that the desire to learn is truly universal.

We are a USA based, 501c3 not for profit corporation working with local communities in developing countries to expand access to quality technology education. We leverage many existing platforms and resources which take the guess work out of learning, allowing us to focus on building communities and organizing events.
