We are firm believers that anyone can learn anything, it just takes time and determination. Putting that belief into practice we are building a community of mentors, learners, and creators in developing countries. We organize bootcamps for schools, weekend events open to the public, and collaborate with other community organizations on the ground to maximize our impact.
