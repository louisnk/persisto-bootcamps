Consistent and supportive environment

Coding fundamentals

Using block-based programming participants can move at their own pace with the help of mentors to learn programming basics. This can take 4-12 weeks depending on at-home practice and builds a great deal of confidence.

Creative cultivation

With the basics down, students are given help and lessons to pursue coding challenges on their own with the support of mentors. They are free to experiment with a range of technologies and get creative to make things that excited them.

Organized course modules

Throughout the year we will plan 4-8 week cohorts to dive deep on a particular topic and give hands on experience using leading edge technology to push the boundaries of what's possible, in the minds of participants and their parents.

How it works

Getting started

  • To ensure we have adequate space and mentorship, we ask all parents wishing to bring a child to register for free
  • Before each event, we will email all registered parents with a link to request tickets
  • Once the available spaces are filled, registrations will be closed
  • Your first visit we will ask you to sign a simple waiver of liability and your student(s) to read and sign an agreement on behavior

What you need

  • A personal email address, which your student can use to register for online learning platform(s) to track progress
  • The ability to bring your student(s) to the designated location from 10am to 1pm, on Saturdays
  • A laptop computer if available*
  • *We maintain a small number of loaner laptops which can be used on a first come first served basis
