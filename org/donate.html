We all need a little help

Our team on the ground is 100% volunteer based and our office is generously provided by the local community. Therefore, 100% of your donations go towards empowering youth to change their path, chase their dreams, and create the future.

If you have an old laptop, tablet, or dollar bill collecting dust, we'd love to put it to good use. Any device less than 7 years old which still functions reasonably well and has a power supply is good enough, batteries not required.

Persisto Learn is registered with the IRS so your donations are tax deductible.
